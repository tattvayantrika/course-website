ANSYS CFD Advanced

Week 1

Refresher, Capacities & Complex Geometry

  • Video 1: Advanced Refresher: CFD Basics, ANSYS Capacities, and Explanation of Mathematical Models.
  • Video 2: Advanced SpaceClaim Features, Including Complex Volume Extraction.
  • Theory Guide: Common Practices for Error-Free Simulations (Model Selection & Meshing).
  • Hands-on Lab: Convergence Plots and Their Interpretation.
  • Weekly Quiz: Test your understanding of advanced model selection and convergence.
Week 2

High-Fidelity Meshing

  • Live Session 1: Interactive Discussion on High-Fidelity Meshing Challenges.
  • Video 1: Advanced Mesh Methods (Biasing, Hexagonal Meshing).
  • Video 2: Localized Refinement: Sphere of Influence & Body of Influence.
  • Theory Guide: Guide to Advanced Meshing Controls.
  • Hands-on Lab: Practice Project 1: Complex Geometry Meshing & Volume Extraction.
  • Weekly Quiz: Validate your grasp of localized refinements and sizing logic.
Week 3

Transient Dynamics

  • Video 1: Transient Flow Inputs and Constituents (What it needs to run properly).
  • Video 2: Transient Flow Simulation Examples: Internal vs. External Flow.
  • Theory Guide: Time-Stepping and Courant Number Guide.
  • Hands-on Lab: Solver Settings for Transient Stability.
  • Weekly Quiz: Test your knowledge on Courant numbers and transient setup rules.
Week 4

Thermal Physics

  • Live Session 2: Thermal Mixing & Heat Exchanger Simulation Review.
  • Video 1: Introduction to Thermal Mixing and Its Simulation.
  • Video 2: Step-by-Step: Steady-State Heat Exchanger Simulation with Explanation.
  • Theory Guide: Thermal Boundary Conditions Reference.
  • Hands-on Lab: Practice Project 2: Heat Exchanger Application.
  • Weekly Quiz: Assess your understanding of thermal boundaries and heat transfer.

Specialized Capabilities

  • Live Session 3: Deep Dive into Multiphase Models and UDF Integration.
  • Video 1: Introduction to Multiphase Simulation & Dynamic Meshing.
  • Video 2: Introduction to UDFs (User Defined Functions) and When to Use Them.
  • Theory Guide: Overview of Multiphase Models (VOF, Eulerian, Mixture).
  • Hands-on Lab: Basic UDF Syntax and Hooking Guide.
  • Weekly Quiz: Evaluate your comprehension of multiphase physics and UDF structures.
Week 6

Validation & Presentation

  • Live Session 4: Final Project Briefing, Validation Checks, and Professional Reporting.
  • Video 1: Residuals and Flux Calculation for Accuracy Checks.
  • Video 2: Presenting the Results Correctly (Viewing results in all possible outputs for professional reporting).
  • Theory Guide: Post-Processing Interpretation for Industrial Reports.
  • Hands-on Lab: Practice Project 3: Transient Flow Application.
  • Final Quiz: Comprehensive End-of-Course Assessment to lock in your mastery.
