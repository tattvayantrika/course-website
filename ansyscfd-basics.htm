ANSYS CFD Basics

Your first step into Computational Fluid Dynamics. In just 4 weeks, build ultimate confidence in the software interface, master basic meshing logic, and successfully simulate simple internal flows.

2 Videos per week 1 Theory Guide & Hands-on Lab English & Hindi 4 Weeks
Enroll in Course
Week 1

Fundamentals & Geometry Preparation

  • Video 1: What is CFD? Where it is used & Intuitive Governing Equations (Continuity & Momentum conceptually).
  • Video 2: Introduction to SpaceClaim Basics & Geometry Import.
  • Revision Guide: Types of Flow (Laminar vs. Turbulent) & Conceptual Theory Cheat Sheet.
Week 2

The Art of Meshing

  • Video 1: What is a Mesh, Why it Matters, and Introduction to the Meshing Interface.
  • Video 2: Understanding Mesh Quality (Skewness, Orthogonality) and How to Check It.
  • Hands-on Lab: Meshing Best Practices & Quality Guidelines Checklist.
Week 3

Software Setup & First Simulation

  • Video 1: Introduction to the ANSYS Fluent UI & Workflow.
  • Video 2: Step-by-Step Simulation Example: 90-Degree Pipe Flow.
  • Revision Guide: Standard Operating Procedure for Setting Up Internal Flows.
Week 4

Results & Practical Application

  • Video 1: Post-Processing Basics (Contours, Vectors, Report Generation).
  • Video 2: Practice Project Walkthrough: Venturimeter Flow Simulation.
  • Hands-on Lab: Venturimeter Project Specifications & Expected Results validation.
